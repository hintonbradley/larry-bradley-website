Larry Bradley

Based in California's Napa Valley and specializing in one of a kind woodworking projects, Larry Bradley has been creating designs and making them come to fruition through his craftmanship talents for over 40 years.

Larry Bradley has completed various projects located throughout the Napa Valley. Some notable companies he's worked with are:

  • Charles Krug Winery
  • Napa Valley Olive Oil Manufacturing Co.
  • St. Helena Junior Women's Club
